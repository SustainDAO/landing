Sustainable Bitcoin Standard (SBS) has created a market-based solution that can help accelerate the Bitcoin network’s transition to clean energy. The objective of SBS is to bring together clean energy bitcoin miners, Institutional investors, energy producers, climate scientists, NGOs, and the bitcoin community into a protocol, designed to address the environmental concerns of the network. SBS believes that Bitcoin powered by clean energy will soon be considered one of our planet’s most sustainable asset classes.

Sustainable Bitcoin Certificates represent “Proof of Sustainable Mining” practices. Due to the nature of the BTC network, each BTC is mined by “all” of the hashpower available on the network, though the reward is attributable only to the first miner that solves the hash function. From a climate change and impact behavioral change perspective, we believe that it’s crucially important to support responsible mining practices using clean energy and create behavioral change to shift the network towards clean energy mining, as the future growth of the network will lead to a critical total energy footprint.

Any investor, institutional or retail, can be an end buyer of SBCs. We believe that SBC’s are a powerful new tool for investors with ESG mandates to utilize in order to put bitcoin on their balance sheet with an assurance of sustainable mining practices. Likewise, we believe that one day centralized exchanges like Coinbase and Gemini will allow retail investors to co-purchase SBC and BTC, helping to drive the bitcoin network towards renewable energy.

SBS’s mission is to help Bitcoin become one of our planet’s most sustainable asset classes. Period.

Sustain Dao token holders can participate directly in the policy-making of the DAO by voting on proposals from our community. Proposals that pass initial votes are then pushed up to Snapshot for weighted governance voting. The Sustain DAO policy team will then implement the community approves policies.
